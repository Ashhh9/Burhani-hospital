About Burhani Hospital

A legacy of trust since 2000

Burhani Hospital is Surat's leading and fastest-growing multi (super) specialty trust hospital, run by Burhani International Medical Association.

Established in 2000 with just 32 beds in the heart of Surat, the hospital quickly earned an outstanding reputation among patients and medical professionals alike. In 2014, we expanded with a new state-of-the-art multispecialty facility to serve the growing healthcare needs of the community.

Today, we stand as a beacon of affordable, high-quality healthcare — combining modern medical technology with compassionate, patient-first service.
